Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026
By using StudyGrove, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

1. Who We Are

StudyGrove is a social study tracking app built to help students track their time, compete with friends, and build consistent study habits. We are an independent project, not a corporation.

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at: supportstudygrove@gmail.com

2. What Data We Collect

When you create an account and use StudyGrove, we collect the following:

  • Your email address — used for login and account recovery
  • Your username — displayed to friends and on the leaderboard
  • Your study stats — total time, streaks, subject hours, achievements
  • Your profile picture — if you choose to upload one
  • Your friend list and group memberships
  • Messages sent in group chats and direct messages

We do not collect your real name, phone number, location, or any payment information.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used strictly to operate the app:

  • To let you log in and sync your progress across devices
  • To show your stats, streak, and achievements
  • To display you on the leaderboard within your group
  • To enable real-time group chat and friend features

We never sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your data for targeted advertising.

4. Third-Party Services

StudyGrove uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Supabase — our database and authentication provider. Your data is stored on their servers. See Supabase's Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel — hosts the StudyGrove web app. See Vercel's Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdSense — we may display ads on the app. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve relevant ads. Google may collect data about your browsing behavior to personalize ads. You can opt out at adssettings.google.com. See Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Cookies & Local Storage

StudyGrove uses your browser's local storage to save your preferences locally (theme, selected frame, notification settings). This data never leaves your device.

Google AdSense may use cookies to personalize and measure ads. By using StudyGrove, you consent to Google's use of cookies for this purpose.

6. Data Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase's infrastructure. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We use secure HTTPS connections for all data transfers.

7. Account Deletion

You can permanently delete your account at any time directly from the app:

  • Go to Settings → Delete Account
  • Confirm by typing DELETE
  • All your data — profile, stats, messages, friendships — will be permanently and immediately removed

If you have trouble deleting your account, contact us at supportstudygrove@gmail.com and we will delete it manually within 48 hours.

8. Children's Privacy

StudyGrove is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the new policy.

10. Contact Us

For any questions, concerns, or data requests, reach us at:
